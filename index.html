Murat KARAKAYA
Associated Professor of Computer Engineering
Atýlým University


I am an Associated Professor at the Department of Computer Engineering at Atýlým University. I received my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer engineering from Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, in 2000 and 2008. 

My main research interests and projects include database management systems, sensor networks, Peer-to-Peer networks, and social networks.


Positions are available for post-doctoral researchers and M.S. and Ph.D. students through externally funded research projects.
