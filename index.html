Murat KARAKAYA

Professor of Computer Engineering
TED University


I am a Professor at the Department of Software Engineering at TED University. I received my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer engineering from Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, in 2000 and 2008. 

My main research interests and projects include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Computing, Sensor Networks, and Peer-to-Peer networks.
