Natural Computing Interest Group

GOAL
        We are a group of people who are interested in solving engineering problems using Natural Computing techniques such as Genetic Algorithms, Ant Colony Optimization, Particle Swarm Optimization,  Bee Colony Optimization, etc.

       The current projects include: data gathering issues in WSN, UAV routing problems, cost optimization, robust algorithms for uncertainty.
